2025 — Breakthroughs Achieved
2025

Vertex Zimislecel Phase 3 enrolls 50 participants

First scalable stem cell therapy for T1D reaches pivotal trial. 10/12 full-dose patients insulin-independent at 1 year.

Nov 2025

Stanford cures T1D in mice without immunosuppression

Hybrid immune system: 19/19 mice protected, 9/9 with established T1D cured via co-transplant approach.

2025

Sana engineered islets survive 6 months in humans

Gene-edited islet cells function without immunosuppression — the holy grail of islet therapy.

2025

Retatrutide: 28.7% weight loss in TRIUMPH-4

Eli Lilly's triple agonist (GLP-1/GIP/Glucagon) sets new efficacy benchmark for T2D.

2025

Chinese team achieves insulin independence with iPSC

Patient's own reprogrammed cells produce insulin — first autologous islet success.

2025

UK Biobank proteomics: 617 T2D-linked proteins identified

Largest proteomic study maps protein-disease network; FAM3D emerges as novel target.

2025

DIAMANTE epigenome: 1,120 CpGs associated with T2D

Multi-ethnic epigenetic analysis reveals CAMK1D, TP53INP1, ATP5G1 regulatory mechanisms.

2025

Abata ABA-201 TCR-Treg therapy enters trials

First T1D-specific TCR-engineered regulatory T cell therapy begins clinical testing.

2025

Inreda bihormonal AP: 80% time-in-range achieved

Fully closed-loop artificial pancreas with insulin + glucagon — no user input needed.

2025

Microbiome-guided nutrition predicts fiber response

RCT shows gut microbiome profiling predicts personalized dietary intervention success in prediabetes.

2025

44% T2D remission with SGLT2i + calorie restriction

Randomized study achieves near-half remission rate with combination approach.

2025

Tzield expanded: PETITE trial in children under 8

First immunotherapy to delay T1D now tested in youngest population yet.

2025

Harvard gut discovery: microbial molecules regulate liver

Gut bacterial metabolites travel to liver and control energy usage — diet-dependent pathway.

2025

Deep RL closed-loop controller: 87.45% TIR

AI-driven fully automated insulin dosing outperforms conventional algorithms in simulation.

2025

GLP-1RAs shown to reduce retinopathy progression 8-15%

Anti-inflammatory effects of GLP-1 drugs extend to retinal protection.
