What This Dashboard Answers

Diabetes research is dense with acronyms — from clinical (HbA1c, OGTT, GAD65) to institutional (NIDDK, JDRF, IDF) to molecular (NLRP3, TXNIP, PPARG). This database provides searchable definitions for every acronym used across the platform, organized by category.

How to Use This

For anyone reading the dashboards: quickly look up unfamiliar abbreviations without leaving the platform. For authors: maintain consistent acronym usage across all content.

What This Cannot Tell You

Acronym definitions are context-specific to diabetes research. Some acronyms have different meanings in other medical specialties. The database covers acronyms used in this platform but is not an exhaustive medical abbreviation reference.
