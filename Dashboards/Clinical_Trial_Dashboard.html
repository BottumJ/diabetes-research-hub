Clinical Trial Intelligence

Diabetes Research Hub · Data: ClinicalTrials.gov API v2 · Snapshot: 2026-03-24 · OSF Registration

What This Dashboard Answers

This dashboard tracks 748+ diabetes clinical trials pulled from ClinicalTrials.gov, covering T1D cure research, immunotherapy, T2D novel therapies, diabetes devices, and recently completed trials. It answers: what is the current state of the diabetes clinical trial pipeline, what phases are trials in, which sponsors are most active, and where are the gaps in trial coverage?

How to Use This

For researchers: identifies active trials to avoid duplication or find collaboration. For patients/advocates: provides a searchable inventory of ongoing trials by condition and phase. For funders: reveals which therapeutic areas are overcrowded and which are underserved.

What This Cannot Tell You

Trial data comes from ClinicalTrials.gov snapshots and may lag behind real-time status changes by days to weeks. Trials registered on non-US registries (EU CTR, ISRCTN) are not captured. Trial quality and likelihood of success are not assessed.

Source: ClinicalTrials.gov REST API v2. Trial counts reflect snapshot date and search criteria; totals may differ from site queries due to filter parameters. Phase "Not Applicable" includes observational studies, device trials without drug phases, and behavioral interventions.
