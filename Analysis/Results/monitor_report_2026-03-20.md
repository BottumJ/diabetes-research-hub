## Breaking News

### Significant developments (past 7 days):

1. **Eli Lilly's orforglipron** — oral GLP-1 candidate — is on track for potential US FDA approval, possibly as early as March 2026. In trials, orforglipron reduced A1C by ~1.3-1.6% and produced ~8% weight loss. This would be the first oral, non-peptide GLP-1 for diabetes.

2. **Novo Nordisk's Awiqli (insulin icodec)** — once-weekly insulin — is under FDA review with approval anticipated in March 2026. This would be the first once-weekly basal insulin option.

3. **Abbott's FreeStyle Libre FreeDM2 study** — presented at ATTD 2026 — demonstrated significant glucose management improvements for T2D patients on basal insulin using CGM technology.

4. **Teplizumab (Tzield) expansion** — previously approved for Stage 2 T1D delay, is expected to gain expanded approval for Stage 3 (newly diagnosed) T1D. Accepted into FDA Commissioner's National Priority Voucher program for accelerated review.

5. **Retatrutide** — Eli Lilly's triple agonist (GIP/GLP-1/glucagon) — NDA submission expected late 2026 with FDA decision projected mid-2027.
