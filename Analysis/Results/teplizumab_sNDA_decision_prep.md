# Teplizumab sNDA Decision Prep — PDUFA April 29, 2026
**Prepared:** 2026-04-03
**Days until decision:** 26
**Drug:** Teplizumab (Tzield) — anti-CD3 monoclonal antibody
**Sponsor:** Sanofi (acquired from Provention Bio)
**Application:** Supplemental NDA for expanded indication
**Current Approval:** Delay onset of Stage 3 T1D in adults and pediatric patients ≥8 years with Stage 2 T1D

---

## What's Being Decided

The sNDA seeks to expand the approved patient population for teplizumab. Based on publicly available information, the expansion likely involves broadening age or staging criteria for T1D delay.

**Key supporting data:**
- PMID 41196293: Safety and pharmacokinetics in children <8 years
- Diabetes Care (Feb 2026): Comprehensive review of disease-modifying potential
- Phase 3 trial data from original approval plus additional post-marketing data
- Active trial NCT07088068 (Sanofi, recruiting)
